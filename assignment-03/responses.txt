What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?
Each device that is connected to the internet has a unique IP address, with the DNS translating domain names to these IP addresse.

What is a cookie?
Cookies are pieces of data that are from a website that is stored within a web browser that the website can then retrieve at a later time.

What are the three essential parts of a URL
The three parts of a URL is the path leading to a specific web page, the HTTP or HTTPS protocol, and the domain name that identifies the site.
