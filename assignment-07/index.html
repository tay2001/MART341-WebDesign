Camp Classic is the go-to summer camp for kids ages 10-18 to hone their technical and musical abilities in the classical sense. Us here at Camp Classic are dedicated to help students grow their musical abilities and have the opportunities to play in larger ensembles and even solo pieces.
Our camp is for the following musicians who play these instruments:

  • Strings (Violin, Viola, Cello, Double Bass)
  • Woodwinds (Flute, Piccolo, Oboe, Bassoon, Clarinet, Saxophone)
  • Brass (Trumpet, French Horn, Trombone, Euphonium, Tuba)
  • Percussion
