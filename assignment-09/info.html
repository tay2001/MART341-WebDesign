Information

Beagles are usually 18 to 35lb in weight and 13 to 16in in height. They have a strong jaw, long soft ears, a somewhat domed skull, and a gumdrop nose.

Beagles have a muscular body with a medium-length, smooth, hard coat.

The beagles white tail tip was bred selectively so that it was a flag for their location when sniffing a scent. Below is the breed's famous tricolor coat.
