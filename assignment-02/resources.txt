What is a directory, and what is its relationship to files?
Information, images, data, etc. is stored in files, which are then stored in directories.
Directories can tore other directories, which form directory trees.
Essentially, directories are used to store, organize, and separate files and/or other directories.

How are VCS useful?
One way VCS are useful because it allows the users to save a project at steps throughout its lifetime.
So, one can roll back to a previous state or even all the way back to the beginning.
You can also compare changes, have other branches, and see who has made changes and when.
